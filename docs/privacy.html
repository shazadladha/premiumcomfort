Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Premium Comfort ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, application, and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and other details you provide when creating an account.
  • TikTok Account Data: When you authorize our application through TikTok, we receive your TikTok user ID, display name, avatar, and basic profile information as permitted by the scopes you authorize (user.info.basic).
  • Content Data: Videos, photos, captions, and other content you choose to post through our Service.
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to interact with TikTok's API on your behalf.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our Service, including posting history and scheduling data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Service.
  • Post content (videos and photos) to TikTok on your behalf using the Content Posting API (video.upload scope).
  • Schedule and manage content posting according to your preferences.
  • Communicate with you about your account and the Service.
  • Improve and optimize our Service.

3. TikTok API Data

Our application uses TikTok's APIs in compliance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service. Specifically:

  • We only access TikTok data that you explicitly authorize through the OAuth consent flow.
  • We use TikTok data solely for the purposes described in this policy (content posting and scheduling).
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your TikTok data with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • You can revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time by visiting your TikTok account settings or by contacting us.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of authentication tokens at rest and in transit.
  • Secure storage of credentials using industry-standard practices.
  • Regular security assessments of our systems.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With TikTok: To post content on your behalf through TikTok's API, as you have authorized.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Premium Comfort, our users, or others.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Revoke Access: Revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

8. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
